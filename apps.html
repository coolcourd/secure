Apps

The apps you use can put you at risk. Even when coming from the App Store or Google Play

Apps have been known to check your location frequently and use it to tailor ads to you.

Some may request access to your contacts or media to sell your contact info or private photos.

They can give you scary looking push notifications to trick you into giving them more information or money.

Lots of apps will give you setting to control how private they keep your information.

You can also control what permissions you give to an application from your phone's settings. Your flashlight app probably doesnt need access to your contacts and media. Your address book app probably does. Do you get the picture?
